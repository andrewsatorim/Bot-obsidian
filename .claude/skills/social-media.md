# Social Media Content

## Platform-Specific Formats

### Twitter / X
```
Hook line (max 280 chars, but aim for 140)

↓ Thread format:
1/ Hook — bold claim or surprising fact
2/ Context — why this matters
3/ Evidence — data, example, screenshot
4/ Insight — what most people miss
5/ Takeaway — actionable advice
6/ CTA — follow, repost, link

[Attach: image, screenshot, or video]
```

**What works:** Hot takes, threads, memes, build-in-public updates, quick tips
**Posting:** 1-3x daily, schedule for 8-10am and 5-7pm user's timezone

### LinkedIn
```
[Hook line — stop the scroll]

[Blank line — creates "see more" click]

Story or insight in 3-5 short paragraphs.
Each paragraph = 1-2 sentences max.

Use line breaks generously.
It feels like breathing room.

Key takeaway or lesson learned.

[CTA: question to spark comments]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3 (3-5 max)
```

**What works:** Career stories, lessons learned, industry insights, contrarian takes, carousel posts
**Posting:** 3-5x per week, Tuesday-Thursday mornings perform best

### Instagram
```
Caption structure:
[Hook — first line visible before "more"]
[Story or value — 2-3 short paragraphs]
[CTA — "Save this for later" / "Tag someone who needs this"]
.
.
.
#hashtags (20-30 relevant, after dot spacers or in comments)
```

**Formats:** Carousels (10 slides, educational), Reels (15-60s, trending audio), Stories (polls, Q&A)
**Posting:** 4-7x per week, 11am-1pm and 7-9pm

### Telegram
```
Channel post:
[Emoji] Bold headline

Key information in 2-3 paragraphs.
Use formatting: **bold**, _italic_, `code`.

• Bullet points for lists
• Keep it scannable
• Link to details

[Button: Read more →]
```

**What works:** Exclusive content, quick updates, behind-the-scenes, community engagement
**Posting:** 1-3x daily, avoid late night

## Content Pillars (Pick 3-5)

| Pillar | Example | Ratio |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Educational** | Tips, tutorials, how-tos | 40% |
| **Behind-the-scenes** | Build process, team, decisions | 20% |
| **Social proof** | Testimonials, milestones, metrics | 15% |
| **Engagement** | Questions, polls, hot takes | 15% |
| **Promotional** | Product launches, offers | 10% |

## Hook Patterns (First Line)

- **Contrarian:** "Stop writing unit tests. Here's why →"
- **Result:** "We grew from 0 to 10K users in 30 days. Here's how:"
- **Mistake:** "I wasted 6 months building the wrong thing."
- **Question:** "What's the #1 skill nobody teaches developers?"
- **List:** "5 tools I use every single day:"
- **Story:** "Last week a customer email changed everything."
- **Curiosity:** "This one change doubled our conversion rate."

## Engagement Rules

1. **Reply to every comment** in the first 60 minutes (algorithm boost)
2. **Ask questions** at the end of posts to invite comments
3. **Engage with others** before and after your post (15 min each)
4. **Never buy followers** — kills engagement rate
5. **Repurpose content** — tweet → LinkedIn → carousel → blog → newsletter
6. **Consistency > frequency** — 3x/week for 6 months beats daily for 3 weeks

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Engagement rate** | Quality of audience interaction |
| **Saves/bookmarks** | Content value (saves > likes) |
| **Profile visits** | Interest in your brand |
| **Link clicks** | Traffic to your product |
| **Follower growth rate** | Momentum, not absolute number |
| **DMs / replies** | Deepest form of engagement |
